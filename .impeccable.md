## Design Context

### Product
Curabo is a white-label medspa and clinic platform for the DACH market. The product spans a public website, a clinic web dashboard, admin/superadmin surfaces, and a mobile patient app. Curabo is the master product brand, while each clinic can layer its own logo, colors, and content on top.

### Users
Primary patient users are German-speaking medspa and aesthetic clinic customers on mobile. They need trust, clarity, calmness, and a premium product feel when browsing treatments, memberships, rewards, and clinic information.

Clinic users are owners, operators, managers, and staff using the dashboard and admin surfaces. They need structure, speed, readability, and confidence more than visual spectacle.

### Brand Personality
Curabo should feel:
- Premium
- Calm
- Precise

Emotional goals:
- Trustworthy enough for medical-aesthetic contexts
- Desirable and polished enough for premium beauty/wellness positioning
- Friendly and clear, never cold or corporate

### Aesthetic Direction
The product should feel like a mature 2026 Apple-grade product with strong visual discipline and high-end desktop/mobile polish.

Core visual direction:
- Light-first interfaces
- White or near-white backgrounds
- Tinted neutrals instead of muddy grayscale
- Strong typography and hierarchy
- Believable depth, subtle inner glow, edge light, and premium surface treatment
- Calm motion with restrained highlights

Reference qualities:
- CleanMyMac clarity and desktop composure
- High-end macOS app polish
- Apple-level attention to spacing, typography, and material quality

This is not a copy of Dermis. Curabo should keep its own identity while matching or exceeding that level of product quality.

### Surface Guidance
Website:
- Editorial, aspirational, premium, credible
- More atmospheric and brand-expressive than admin surfaces

Dashboard and admin:
- Structured, clear, fast, desktop-quality
- Lower visual flamboyance than the mobile app
- Strong information hierarchy and readable data presentation

Mobile app:
- The most brand-forward surface
- Premium, luminous, polished, object-like UI
- Calm and trustworthy first, expressive second

White-label behavior:
- Clinic branding should personalize the shell without degrading the product system
- Curabo remains the underlying design language
- A clinic logo or accent color should never break spacing, contrast, or component quality

### Design Principles
1. Clarity before decoration.
2. Function before effects.
3. Strong hierarchy, confident typography, and readable contrast.
4. Premium surfaces with believable depth, not fake glass.
5. Consistent spacing rhythm across all screens.
6. Motion should feel quiet, supportive, and intentional.
7. White-label branding must sit inside one coherent Curabo product system.

### Anti-Patterns
Avoid:
- Cheap glassmorphism
- Washed-out grey typography
- Purple-on-dark AI-default aesthetics
- Generic SaaS card spam
- Dribbble-style overdesign
- Flat, timid, low-contrast interfaces
- Random shadows, inconsistent radii, and uneven spacing
- Playful or gimmicky motion

### Accessibility
- Prioritize clear readability and strong contrast in light mode
- Respect reduced-motion expectations
- Keep tap targets and controls comfortably sized
- Never rely on color alone to communicate state
